About RGC

RGC was founded by DeuceyBoy after unlocking the rare MVP++ dark blue rank. Alongside Freshlo, they created RGC to give away ranks in a fun, interactive way.

"We wanted to make someone's day, build a community, and have a good reason to log in and play with friends!"

How It Works

  1. Check announcements on Discord
  2. Join the Hypixel Housing on the event day/time
  3. Compete in mini-games and challenges
  4. Win ranks, cosmetics, or other prizes!

Meet the Team

Role Definitions
  • Admin - A senior-level member of the All-Around Staff, with the same responsibilities as All-Around Staff.
  • Staff Manager - In charge of all lower staff roles with the help of the Co-Owner.
  • Event Staff - Moderates the housing world during events.
  • Discord Staff - Moderates the Discord Server.
  • Developer Manager - In charge of all Developers and development projects, helps code for the housing world.
  • Developer - Codes for the housing world.
  • Builder Manager - In charge of all Builders and building projects, helps build for the housing world.
  • Builder - Builds all necessary event areas for the housing world.
  • Staff (All-Around) - Helps moderate Discord, events, and either builds or codes for the housing.
  • Helper - Similar to Event Staff, but with less responsibility.
  • Host - A temporary role assigned to whoever is currently hosting an event, if not a managing staff member.
  • Trial Staff - A new staff member who could be working toward Event Staff, Discord staff, Helper, Builder, or Developer.
