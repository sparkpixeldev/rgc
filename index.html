About RGC

RGC was founded by DeuceyBoy after unlocking the rare dark blue MVP++ rank. Alongside Freshlo, they created RGC to give away ranks in a fun, interactive way.

"We wanted to make someone's day, build a community, and have a good reason to log in and play with friends!"

How It Works

  1. Check announcements on Discord
  2. Join the Hypixel Housing on the event day/time
  3. Compete in mini-games and challenges
  4. Win ranks, cosmetics, or other prizes!

Meet the Team

Role Definitions
Admin

A senior-level member of the All-Around Staff, with the same responsibilities as All-Around Staff.

Staff Manager

In charge of all lower staff roles with the help of the Co-Owner.

Event Staff

Moderates the housing world during events.

Discord Staff

Moderates the Discord Server.

Developer Manager

In charge of all Developers and development projects, helps code for the housing world.

Developer

Codes for the housing world.

Builder Manager

In charge of all Builders and building projects, helps build for the housing world.

Builder

Builds all necessary event areas for the housing world.

Staff (All-Around)

Helps moderate Discord, events, and either builds or codes for the housing.

Helper

Similar to Event Staff, but with less responsibility.

Host

A temporary role assigned to whoever is currently hosting an event, if not a managing staff member.

Trial Staff

A new staff member who could be working toward Event Staff, Discord staff, Helper, Builder, or Developer.

FAQ

RGC was founded by DeuceyBoy after unlocking the rare MVP++ dark blue rank. Alongside Freshlo, they created RGC to give away ranks in a fun, interactive way.

RGC stands for Rank Gifting Competitions.

Check the announcements on our Discord for event details. On the day of the event, join the specified Hypixel Housing using the command provided in the announcement.

Staff applications open periodically. Keep an eye on the #announcements channel in our Discord for information on when applications are open and how to apply.

You can contact staff through our Discord server, either by pinging a staff role in relevant channels or by opening a support ticket.

Boosting our Discord server grants special perks! Check the #boost-perks channel in Discord for a full list of benefits.

Information about becoming a Gifter (someone who donates ranks/prizes) can be found in our Discord or by contacting leadership.
