Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

TL;DR: We don't use Google Analytics or any big trackers. We don't sell your data. This site just collects basic, anonymous visit info to keep things running smoothly.

📋 1. What We Collect

When you visit our site, we collect basic, anonymous info like your general region, what type of device you're on, and whether you're using a VPN. That's about it.

We don't collect your name, email, or anything that identifies you personally. No accounts or logins are needed to use this site.

⚙️ 2. How We Collect It

We use our own small analytics script, not a third-party tracker. No cookies, no fingerprinting, no cross-site tracking.

🎯 3. Why We Collect It

Just the basics: keeping the site safe from spam, making sure pages load well on the devices people actually use, and seeing which parts of the world our community is growing in so we can plan events better.

🔗 4. Third Parties

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages, so GitHub handles the hosting side of things.

We link out to Discord, Twitch, and YouTube. Once you click one of those links, you're on their platform and their privacy policies apply, so we don't control what they do.

🛡️ 5. Children's Privacy

We know a lot of our visitors are younger, as it's a Minecraft community! We don't sell or share anyone's data, period. Any info collected is used strictly for the internal operation of the site (keeping things running, preventing spam). If a parent or guardian has questions, feel free to reach out on our Discord.
